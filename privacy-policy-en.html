Privacy Policy

Case Simulator for Standoff 2

Last updated: December 18, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Case Simulator for Standoff 2 (hereinafter referred to as "Application", "Game", "we", "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have regarding your data.

Important: Our application is an offline simulator without a server component. All game data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

2. What Data We Collect

2.1. Data Stored Locally on Device

The following data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to us:

  • Player Profile: nickname, avatar, level, game experience
  • Game Currency: gold balance
  • Inventory: received items, cases, skins
  • Statistics: number of opened cases, mini-game wins
  • Achievements: achievement completion progress
  • Settings: sound, music, vibration, language

2.2. Automatically Collected Data

The following data is automatically collected through third-party services when using the application:

Data Type Purpose Service
Device Identifier Analytics and Advertising Yandex AppMetrica
Device Information (model, OS) App Optimization Yandex AppMetrica
In-app Events User Experience Improvement Yandex AppMetrica
Advertising Identifier (IDFA) Showing Relevant Ads Yandex Mobile Ads
Installation Data (SKAdNetwork) Install Attribution Ad Networks

3. How We Use Data

  • Local Data: used exclusively for the game to function on your device
  • Analytics: helps us understand how users interact with the app and improve it
  • Advertising: allows us to display ads and keep the app free

4. Advertising and App Tracking Transparency

Our application contains advertising from Yandex Mobile Ads. In accordance with Apple's requirements, we request your permission for tracking through App Tracking Transparency (ATT).

Your Choice: You can decline tracking when prompted. This will not affect the app's functionality, but ads may be less relevant to your interests.

You can change your choice at any time:

Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Case Simulator

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Yandex Mobile Ads — ad display (Privacy Policy)
  • Yandex AppMetrica — analytics (Terms of Use)

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete All Data: use the "Reset Progress" function in app settings
  • Opt Out of Tracking: decline the ATT request or change settings in iOS
  • Delete the App: upon deletion, all local data will be erased

8. Children

Our application is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

9. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. For significant changes, we will notify you through an app update. We recommend checking this page periodically.
